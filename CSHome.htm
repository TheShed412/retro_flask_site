Welcome! This is the site of the official CCAC Boyce Computer Science Club.
Here we are dedicated to the study and understanding of the computational sciences.

Our work that we have done will be on the left. In the News page will be weekly updates on the progress of certain projects we have been working on.

The Robot page will be dedicated to the progression of CSBot.

The Blog page will be updates made by the members of the CS club about what their thoughts on what is happening and what they might be working on.

The Games page will be the games we have developed in JavaScript that will run on the unique "CSDOS" window. This will provide a unique expierience to in browser gaming.

The Projects page will be the projects in the works, be it applications, games we are working on, algorithms we are studying, or more robot stuff because robots are awesome.
